### Modern Networking - Fundamental Concepts
This repository is a companion to the [main website](https://modern-networking.com) for the book _Modern Networking - Fundamental Concepts_. Here you'll find [FAQs](faqs.md) with answers that expand on or clarify topics from the book. The list will be updated regularly and other bonus material may be added in future.

If you have a question that isn't covered in the FAQs, or have other feedback on the book you'd like to share, please reach out — either directly through the book's main website, or open an issue here — we'd love to hear from you!
